Ingredients:
	1 stalk of Lemon Grass, cut in 1-in. pieces
	2 can (14oz) coconut milk
	2 can (14oz) reduced-sodium chicken broth
	12 quarter size slices fresh ginger
	1 lb boned, skinned chicken breast or thighs, cut into 1-in. chunks
	1 cup sliced mushrooms
	4 tbspn fresh lime juice
	4 tbspn Thai or Vietnamese fish sauce (nuoc mam or nam pla)
	2 tbspn sugar
	2 tbspn Thai chili paste(optional)

In a medium saucepan, combine coconut milk, broth, ginger, and lemongrass and bring to boil over high heat. Add chicken, mushrooms, lime juice, fish sauce, sugar, and chili paste. Reduce heat and simmer until chicken is firm and opaque, 5 to 10 minutes. Discard lemongrass.
